Process

From call to shipped work.

The bot turns messy product conversations into an approved plan, then hands the team a clean implementation queue.

01

Join the call

Paste a Meet link and send the notetaker into the room with the right Slack destination.

02

Draft the PRD

Deepgram captures the discussion, then Gemini shapes the transcript into a structured PRD.

03

Approve tickets

Review the PRD in Slack, approve the plan, and create Linear tickets from the agreed scope.

04

Ship the work

Codex can pick up the Linear tasks, deliver the changes, and close the loop.
